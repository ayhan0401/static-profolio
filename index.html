  • Zelda 1986 First Dungeon Remake & Custom Level

    Technology Used: C#, Unity

    This project is a remake of the first dungeon from Zelda 1986, along with a custom level featuring a brand-new weapon. Worked in a team of two.

    My Contributions:

    • Implemented grid-like movement.
    • Developed weapons: sword, boomerang, bomb, and bow and arrow.
    • Designed and implemented the weapon and collectable UI.
    • Added functional player aesthetics for direction and movement.
    • Implemented room transitions.
    • Created locked doors, keys, and all rooms in the first dungeon (including bow room and old-man room).
    • Integrated audio for the main level and custom level.

    Custom Level - New Weapon Concept:

    • Designed a new teleport gun.
    • The teleport gun shoots out a clone of Link in the direction he is facing.
    • The clone moves until stopped by a wall, the edge of a room, or an enemy/trap.
    • If hit by an enemy or trap, the clone disappears.
    • If stopped by the edge of the room, a second tap of the attack button teleports Link to the clone��s position.
    • Can be used to bait out traps or reach previously inaccessible areas.

  • Turn-Based Chess-Inspired Puzzle Game

    Technology Used: C#, Unity

    Made by myself. It is a strategy puzzle game inspired by chess and Chinese chess, where players must strategically swap control between pieces to reach the goal before an enemy piece following a predetermined path.

    Key Point:

    • Implemented a unique mechanic allowing the player to switch control to another piece upon collision, dynamically updating movement patterns.
    • Designed and balanced levels to introduce movement patterns through enemy demonstration before player interaction.
    • Refined the turn-based feel by adding a slight pause between moves, a turn-indicator UI, and movement sound effects.
    • Enhanced game aesthetics by reworking floor textures to better fit the chess theme and adding a glow effect to the end goal.
